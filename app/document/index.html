Katen - Laravel Minimalism Blog & Magazine ​

Introduction ​

Katen is a multipurpose Laravel Minimalism Blog & Magazine. This script comes with 5 high-quality pre-build home pages and many built-in awesome features.

It is built on top of Botble CMS, which is a Laravel-based CMS. It offers remarkable flexibility for various use cases.
